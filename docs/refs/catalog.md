## Multi-angle synthesis (what the combined set tells the model)

**Ulugh Beg (west)** — frontal, 3-quarter, looking-up-into-iwan, minaret close.
- Tall pishtaq; deep pointed iwan with a real **muqarnas (honeycomb) half-vault**.
- Tympanum = girih **star constellation** mosaic; buff brick + cobalt geometric.
- **Slender** minarets, banded girih, **corbelled gallery** crown (famously leaned).

**Sher-Dor (east)** — frontal, 3-quarter, spandrel close, dome+minaret tops.
- **Tiger + sun-face** spandrels (gold/ochre on cobalt) over the iwan.
- **Two ribbed turquoise domes** on tall kufic drums; slender minarets w/ galleries.

**Tilya-Kori (north)** — frontal, 3-quarter, dome close, gilded interior, corner cupola.
- Symmetric long **two-storey pointed-arch arcades**; central pishtaq w/ **gold rosettes**.
- One big **bright-turquoise ribbed dome** on a tall drum; **corner towers w/ small cupolas**.

**Overall / plaza** — axial frontal, oblique, aerial-ish, night, interiors, models.
- Warm buff stone dominates; cobalt+turquoise+gold accents; pale daytime sky.
- U-plan open to the south; warm stone paving, gardens, fountains.
